David Miguel Gray

I am an Associate Professor of Philosophy and an affiliate of the Institute for Intelligent Systems at the University of Memphis. My work sits at the intersection of language, cognition, and artificial intelligence. I focus on whether syntactic and semantic structures are encoded in the embedding spaces of large language models, and whether those structures play a causal role in generating model outputs. Current projects involve empirical methodologies such as structural probes, causal interventions, and artificial language training to evaluate linguistic competence and interpretability in modern LLMs.
